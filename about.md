Hi, I'm **Roberto Cornacchia**. 

By day, I am a co-founder of [Spinque](https://www.spinque.com/), a company we spun off from the Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) in Amsterdam (where I also got my PhD). As an Information Retrieval and database specialist, I work at the intersection of high-performance databases and search technology.

By night (and sometimes over long weekends), I dance Lindy Hop and build slightly mad side projects to see how far I can stretch technology. 

My blog, **SwingBit**, is named after these two passions: *swinging bits* in code, and a *bit of swing* on the dance floor.
